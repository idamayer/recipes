# Grandma Selma's Sweet Potato Casserole

> My grandmother's famous sweet potato casserole, although the original recipe is scaled to feed 33 people in her massive annual Thanksgiving events.

## Ingredients

- 5½ cups baked sweet potatoes, peeled and mashed (about 7)
- 1¼ sticks clarified butter, melted
- 1¼ cups sugar
- ¾ teaspoon cinnamon
- ¾ teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
- 4½ eggs, beaten
- 2 tablespoons Jack Daniel's
- 2 tablespoons Grand Marnier
- 1⅛ cups milk
- 1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1⅛ teaspoons grated orange rind

---

## Method

### 01 — Prepare the Sweet Potatoes

Bake the sweet potatoes until completely tender. While still hot, peel and mash until very smooth, removing any fibrous strings. Stir in the melted butter.

### 02 — Mix the Flavorings

In a separate bowl, combine the sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, orange rind, and lemon extract.

### 03 — Make the Custard

Beat the eggs until creamy. Gradually add the sugar and spice mixture, then mix in the Jack Daniel's, Grand Marnier, milk, and vanilla extract.

### 04 — Combine

Fold the custard mixture into the sweet potatoes and beat until fully incorporated and smooth.

### 05 — Bake

Transfer to a well-greased baking dish. Bake at 350°F (175°C) until set but still creamy in the center, approximately 45–60 minutes depending on the depth of the dish.
