## Ingredients

- 4 frozen tilapia fillets, or as many fit in a single layer on your steaming basket
- Cilantro
- Soy sauce marinated lupini beans or fermented soy beans *(optional)*

---

## Method

### 01 — Prepare Equipment

Line a steamer basket and make sure you have a pan or pot that fits perfectly underneath it, filled with water that you will bring to a boil before setting the basket on to steam.

If using lupini beans, optionally shallow fry them for extra texture or just drain them.

### 02 — Steam the Fish

Lay your frozen tilapia in a single layer. They can be frozen or thawed; it doesn’t matter.

Bring the water to a boil and steam the fish. Check on the fish after around 8 minutes until it flakes when pressed with a fork. Ideally cook until just done, then transfer to a large platter.

### 03 — Prepare the Garnish and Sauce

Meanwhile wash and chop cilantro.

Mix all the remaining sauce ingredients together and warm in a pan or microwave until just hot enough to melt the sugar.

### 04 — Finish the Dish

Arrange the fish on the platter, place beans on top if using, and top that with a single pile of cilantro.

Bring the oil to temperature until almost smoking or just really hot and pour the hot oil over the cilantro tower. The cilantro will naturally spread but you can distribute it more evenly if needed.

Pour sauce on top and serve.
