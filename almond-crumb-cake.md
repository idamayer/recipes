## Ingredients

### Crumb
- 2½ cups minus 1 Tbsp unbleached all-purpose flour
- 1¼ cups black sesame powder
- 1 cup sugar *(reduced from 1¼ cups)*
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 cup (2 sticks, 8 ounces) butter, melted
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

### Cake
- 8 tablespoons (1 stick, 4 ounces) butter
- ¾ cup sugar *(reduced from 1 cup)*
- 2 large eggs
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 3/4 teaspoon almond extract
- 1 cup (8 ounces) sour cream
- 2 cups (8½ ounces) unbleached all-purpose flour
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon baking powder

---

## Method

### 01 — Prep and Preheat
Preheat the oven to 350°F. Grease the preferred pan(s). The recipe will make two 8-inch round coffeecakes, or fill a 9 x 13-inch pan, or a 9- or 10-inch tube pan.

### 02 — To Make the Crumb
In a medium-sized mixing bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, black sesame powder, reduced sugar, salt, and cinnamon. Melt the butter in the microwave or small saucepan and add the extracts to it. Pour the butter into the flour mixture and mix until all the butter is absorbed and you have a uniformly moistened crumb mixture. Set aside while you make the cake batter.

### 03 — To Make the Batter
In a large mixing bowl, cream the butter and reduced sugar until light and fluffy. Add the eggs one at a time, and beat between additions. Scrape down the mixing bowl, then beat in the vanilla and sour cream. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk the flour, baking soda, salt, and baking powder together. Add to the butter/sour cream mixture, mixing until evenly combined.

### 04 — Assembly and Baking
Pour the batter into the greased baking pan(s). Crumble the black sesame crumb mixture over the top, until the batter is completely covered. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes for 8-inch rounds, 30 to 35 minutes for a 9 x 13-inch pan, or 35 to 40 minutes for a 9- or 10-inch tube pan. Bake until a tester inserted in the center comes out clean. Remove the cake from the oven and cool on a rack; dust the top with confectioners' sugar, if desired.

## A Note from the Kitchen

> This version has a black sesame crumble on top. The original included 1Tbsp more flour as well as cinnamon and 3/4 tsp almond extract. I've also reduced the overall sugar.
